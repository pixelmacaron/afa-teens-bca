Walk to Fight Alzheimer’s

On Saturday, October 13, 2024, AFA attended the WalktoFight Alzheimer’s event in Paramus at Bergen Community College. Over 500 walkers participated in this event, raising a total of over $150,000 alongside 17 other sponsors. During our time there, we were able to speak with different assisted living homes and connect with various Alzheimer’s Research foundations, which helped us understand what fighting this disease meant to many different people. At the end of the walk, many inspiring speakers, including the mayor of Paterson, took the stage and shared their personal touching stories about losing special loved ones who have succumbed to Alzheimer’s. We were deeply inspired and moved by these stories and how supportive and strong the Alzheimer’s community in Bergen County has become over the years.
